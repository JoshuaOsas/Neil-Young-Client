Welcome to Trust Fund Winnipeg Foundation

Together, we can support Neil Young's journey to recovery

At Trust Fund Winnipeg Foundation, we understand the profound impact that community support can have on individuals in need. Our mission is to rally together to assist Neil Young in his fight for health, specifically for his small intestine surgery. With a dedicated team and a heartfelt commitment to making a difference, we work tirelessly to ensure that every contribution helps improve lives and fosters hope.

Whether you're looking to donate, volunteer, or spread the word, your involvement is crucial. Join us in making a meaningful change and be part of a compassionate movement that prioritizes healing and support for those we care about.

Ways you can contribute

From donations to fundraising events, we invite you to participate in various initiatives that directly support Neil Young's medical needs.
Experience the power of community as we unite for a common cause.

Our Donation Drive is designed to gather essential funds that will go directly towards Neil Young's surgery and recovery. Every contribution, no matter the size, plays a vital role in ensuring he receives the care he needs.

Participate in our Fundraising Events, where we organize community gatherings and activities aimed at raising awareness and funds for Neil's medical journey. Together, we can support a worthy cause.

Spread the word through our Awareness Campaigns. Help us reach more people by sharing information about Neil Young's situation and the impact of your support. Together, we can amplify our message and drive more contributions.
